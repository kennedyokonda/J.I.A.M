Grow Your Faith

We are so excited for you to start your journey of faith. Here are a few ways you can begin to grow in your walk with Christ.

WAYS TO GROW

Our desire is to see you grow and discover your full potential in Christ in every area of your life.

KidsLife (10 years and Below)

KidsLife is a place where kids experience that God is a great big God and they can have a great big life! We love partnering with parents to help kids from birth to fifth grade grow in their walk with God and develop a lasting relationship with him. For in person services, registration is required for childcare and children 1st Grade and older are required to wear a mask. Children Kindergarten and under are encouraged to wear a mask.

Financial Classes

The J.I.A.M Financial Ministry exists to encourage, equip and empower J.I.A.M members as well as our local community to live out Biblical financial principles to experience true financial freedom. Financial Freedom Class – Virtual: Sundays at 4 PM Central on Facebook and Zoom  In Person: Sundays 10:15am - 4th floor,   Financial Ministry Social Media Pages Financial Ministry - Business Ministry - Financial Freedom and Business Ministry LifeGroups:

Marriage Classes

The J.I.A.M Marriage Ministry exists to provide powerful resources that help strengthen the relationships of those who are dating, engaged, or married. We offer opportunities to connect with other couples through engaging classes, lifegroups, retreats, and conferences that will propel you toward a lasting marriage legacy.

Monday Night Bible Study

Join us every Monday night for an extended look together into the Bible, taught by magretwanjiru, Timithy and more! This powerful time of exploring the Bible will strengthen your faith and walk with Jesus.

LEADERSHIP PROGRAM

Be the leader you’re meant to be. Check out the J.I.A.M leadership classes, offering church-work training as well as business leadership programs designed to help you fulfill your calling and impact your community.

Men's Classes

The Men’s Ministry helps men grow in their relationship with God and connect in community. As men are impacted, their lives change for the better in every area.

Women's Classes

We believe that we are Better Together! J.I.A.M Women's Ministry is designed to be a place to connect and share, to be rejuvenated, inspired and equipped. No matter what age or state of life you're in, we have a place for you to laugh, love and learn with us!

NextGen

he Next Gen Ministry exists to give hope to the NEXT generation of J.I.A.M Church, calling and equipping youth and young adults to follow Jesus Christ. The new Next Gen service happens once a month on the first Wednesday of each month in the Main Auditorium at 7pm.

Young at Heart (Seniors)

Young at Heart is designed to enhance the lives of J.I.A.M married and single senior adults. We provide a setting where you can develop lasting friendships, strengthen your faith, share your talents, and stay active and healthy!

Champions Club

Champions Club is designed for children, youth and adults with special needs, and ministers to them spiritually, intellectually and physically. Our unique curriculum, designed by professionals in various fields of special needs care, allows each person to be ministered to at their individual level.

Find Your Class

Whether you are looking to be a better spouse, need support creating a financial plan, or need support overcoming an addiction, we have a class for you.

Visit the J.I.A.M Store

The J.I.A.M Bookstore aims to strengthen your walk with Christ through providing resources that will inform and encourage you. Check out the latest messages and books from Pastor Joel, worship from J.I.A.M Music, and so much more!
